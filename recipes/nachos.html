Nachos

These Nachos are a quick and easy dinner or weekend snack. Just like when making tacos, its best to put your favorite garnishes on these nachos. The ingredients listed below happen to be my families favorite garnishes so that is why they are listed. If you are serving these nachos to children who don't care for spicy food, it's best to take their portion of the meat out before adding the tomatos and green chilis. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Taco Seasoning Packet
  • 1 Small Can of Diced Tomatos and Green Chili
  • 4 Tbsp Velveeta

Optional Garnishes

  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Sour Cream
  • Chipotle Sauce
  • Salsa Verde
  • Guacamole
  • Tortilla Chips
  • Anything you want!

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  2. Drain fat.
  3. Add taco seasoning packet and simmer according to package instructions.
  4. Add Velveeta and melt into seasoned meat.
  5. Add small can of diced tomatos and green chilis.
  6. Add tortilla chips to bowl and fan chips around bowl.
  7. Spoon meat into center of bowl.
  8. Top meat and chips with optional garnishes.
  9. Enjoy!
