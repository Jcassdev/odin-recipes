Shephard's Pie

Another easy dinner. I prefer to use instant mashed potatoes and frozen mixed vegetables. If you prefer to peel and mash your own potatoes go right ahead! There's no wrong way to make this dish. The whole point is to make it how you want to. I prefer the quick and easy method!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Small Can of Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 Small Can of Water
  • 1 Package of Frozen Mixed Vegetables
  • Instant Mashed Potatoes
  • 2 Tbsp Worchestershire Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook 1 lb of ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  2. Drain fat.
  3. Add 1 half of a small can of tomato paste.
  4. Add water to empty half of small can and pour into meat and tomato paste mixture.
  5. Add 2 Tbsp of worchestershire sauce.
  6. Mix well.
  7. Simmer over low-medium heat until mixture thickens.
  8. Take off heat.
  9. Prepare instant mashed potatoes and frozen mixed vegetables as per package directions.
  10. In a small caserole dish add meat and spread out to cover bottom of dish.
  11. Add vegetables and spread out in layer over meat.
  12. Add mashed potatoes and layer over vegetables.
  13. Put caserole dish in oven and broil until the top of the mashed potatoes becomes slightly hardened and slightly golden.
  14. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  15. Enjoy!
